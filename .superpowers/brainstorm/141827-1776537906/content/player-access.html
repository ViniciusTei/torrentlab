Where should the video player open from?

Pick how the user gets from a download to watching the video

A

Downloads page → Play button

Each completed (and in-progress) item on the Downloads page gets a "▶ Assistir" button. Clicking opens a full-screen player page at /player/:infoHash.

Pros

  • Centralised — one place to manage and watch
  • Works naturally for in-progress downloads too

Cons

  • User must navigate away from movie detail
B

Movie detail page → "Assistir" button

When a movie is downloaded (or downloading), a "▶ Assistir" button appears on its detail page alongside the torrent list. Opens the same player page.

Pros

  • Natural flow: find movie → click watch
  • Shows download badge + play in one spot

Cons

  • Requires knowing which movie page to visit
C

Both — Downloads page AND movie detail page

Play button on the Downloads page cards AND on the movie detail page when download exists. Same player destination.

Pros

  • Maximum discoverability

Cons

  • Slightly more implementation surface
